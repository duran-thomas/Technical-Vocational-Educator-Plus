About Us

A tradition of excellence for over a decade.The Technical & Vocational Educator Plus (TVEP) became a stunning success, enrolling more than 500 students in its first decade. As the years progressed, more students would choose The Technical & Vocational Educator Plus. By the beginning of 2016, 1,300 students had enrolled in The Technical & Vocational Educator Plus programs, and as of today that number has has increased to 1500.

We owe our success to all our industrious, dedicated & well invested tutors.TVEP started out with only four students, but the numbers rapidly increased as the performance of these four students were impressive and sparked interest among many parents. Before we knew it, we had to seek a larger location to host all the interested students. We're proud of our history and excited about our future. We look forward to helping you become a successful part of our legacy and accomplishment.
